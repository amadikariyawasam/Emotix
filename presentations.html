Project Slide Decks

Four key presentations delivered across the academic year — from initial proposal to final defense.

01

Proposal Presentation

Introduces the EMOTIX project concept, the identified research gap in employee wellness monitoring, objectives, proposed methodology, and the expected outcomes of the AI-based system. Delivered to the research supervisory panel for approval.

⬇ Download Slides 👁 View Slides
02

Progress Presentation I

Showcases early-stage implementation results — including the initial edge data collection module, preliminary stress detection models, and eye-tracking proof-of-concept. Demonstrates technical progress against project milestones and objectives.

⬇ Download Slides 👁 View Slides
03

Progress Presentation II

Presents the near-complete integrated EMOTIX system with all four components — stress detection, eye tracking, routine monitoring, and chatbot. Includes performance benchmarks, accuracy metrics, and security test results from the AES-256-GCM and honeypot modules.

⬇ Download Slides 👁 View Slides
04

Final Presentation

The comprehensive final defense of the EMOTIX research project. Covers the complete system architecture, experimental results, comparative analysis with existing literature, contributions to knowledge, limitations, and future work recommendations.
